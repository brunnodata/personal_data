TECHSTORE PLUS — SERVICE CATALOG
TITLE: Available Services and Coverage
Document ID: SVC-CAT-001
Effective: January 2024

================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

TechStore Plus offers the following services for products purchased through its
channels. Services are sold separately from the product unless noted. This catalog
describes eligibility, pricing, and coverage for each service.

For warranty terms, see policy_warranty_terms.txt (current, v3.0) or
policy_warranty_terms_2023.txt (archived, v2.0, for purchases before 2024).
For trade-in credits and upgrade paths, see trade_in_program.txt.

================================================================================
SERVICE 1 — TECHNICAL SUPPORT
================================================================================

Name: Technical Support (TechAssist)
Availability: All TechStore Plus hardware products
Cost:
  - First 90 days from purchase: FREE (included with every TechStore Plus product)
  - After 90 days (without warranty extension): $29 per incident
  - After 90 days (with Standard Warranty Extension, Tier 2): $29 per incident
    (Standard tier does not include post-90-day free technical support)
  - After 90 days (with Premium Protection Plan, Tier 3): INCLUDED at no charge
    Technical support is included as a benefit of the Premium Protection Plan for
    the full duration of the plan (36 months). This applies to Laptop Pro X1,
    WorkStation Z, and Router NX300.

Coverage:
  - Phone, email, and chat support (1-800-TECH-PLUS, support@techstoreplus.com)
  - Remote diagnostics (screen share session for laptop and desktop products)
  - Firmware guidance (Router NX300, Smart Hub Home)
  - Setup assistance for Smart Hub Home mesh pairing with Router NX300
  - Driver and software troubleshooting (Windows 11, app issues)

NOT covered by Technical Support (any tier):
  - Third-party software issues (non-TechStore Plus apps)
  - Data recovery
  - Network infrastructure issues beyond TechStore Plus devices

SERVICE 2 — EXTENDED WARRANTY ADD-ON (1 YEAR)
================================================================================

Name: Extended Warranty Add-On
Availability: Premium tier products ONLY
  - Laptop Pro X1 (SKU: LP-X1-2024)
  - WorkStation Z (SKU: WS-Z-2024)
  - Router NX300 (SKU: RT-NX300-2024)

The Extended Warranty Add-On is NOT available for Standard tier products (Laptop
Air S2, Smart Hub Home) or Basic tier products (Laptop Lite V3, Smart Cam 4K).
Standard tier customers should instead purchase the Standard Warranty Extension
(described in policy_warranty_terms.txt) within 90 days of purchase.

Cost: $49 flat fee per product per year
Duration: Extends Premium Protection Plan by 12 additional months (from 36 months
          to 48 months total)
Coverage: Same as Premium Protection Plan (Tier 3) — hardware failure, accidental
          damage, water damage (as of v3.0, 2024 terms)
Eligibility window: Must be purchased before the original 36-month Premium plan expires.
Limit: Maximum 2 add-on years (48 months + 12 + 12 = maximum 60 months total coverage).

================================================================================
SERVICE 3 — HOME INSTALLATION SERVICE
================================================================================

Name: Home Installation Service
Cost: $49 flat fee (one-time, per installation session)
Duration: 2-hour on-site visit by a certified TechStore Plus technician.

Available for the following products:
  - Router NX300 (SKU: RT-NX300-2024)
    Technician will: physically install the router in the optimal location, configure
    the Setup Wizard, set up WPA3 security, configure ISP connection type, and
    optionally pair with Smart Hub Home for mesh networking.
  - Smart Hub Home (SKU: SH-HOME-2023)
    Technician will: connect the Smart Hub Home to the Router NX300 (or Router NX200)
    via Ethernet, configure the TechStore SmartHome app, enable mesh networking,
    and pair up to 5 initial smart devices (Zigbee, Z-Wave, or Wi-Fi).
  - Smart Cam 4K (SKU: SM-CAM-4K)
    Technician will: mount the Smart Cam 4K in a customer-selected location, connect
    to the Smart Hub Home or Router NX300 network, and configure motion alerts.

NOT available for: Laptop Pro X1, Laptop Air S2, Laptop Lite V3, WorkStation Z,
or any accessory product. Those products use self-guided setup manuals.

Booking: techstoreplus.com/installation or 1-800-TECH-PLUS
Available Mon-Fri 9:00-17:00, Sat 10:00-14:00, within 50 miles of TechStore Plus
service centers.

================================================================================

Name: Trade-In Program
Details: See trade_in_program.txt for complete terms, eligible products, and credit

SERVICE 5 — FINANCING (0% APR, 12 MONTHLY INSTALLMENTS)
================================================================================

Name: TechStore Plus Financing
Provider: TechStore Plus Financial Services (in partnership with First Capital Bank)
Cost: $0 interest (0% APR) — total paid equals purchase price
Eligibility: Purchases over $500. Subject to credit approval.
Terms: 12 equal monthly payments. No early repayment penalty.

Eligible products for financing:
  - Any single purchase over $500, including:
    - Laptop Pro X1 ($1,299) — eligible
    - Laptop Air S2 ($799) — eligible
    - WorkStation Z ($3,299) — eligible
    - Router NX300 ($199) — NOT eligible alone (under $500)
    - Home Office Bundle ($1,379) — eligible
    - Creator Bundle ($3,599) — eligible
  - Financing CAN be combined with trade-in credits (trade-in reduces financed total)

Examples:
  Laptop Pro X1 ($1,299) / 12 months = $108.25/month
  WorkStation Z ($3,299) / 12 months = $274.92/month
  Laptop Air S2 ($799) / 12 months = $66.58/month
  Laptop Air S2 trade-in ($200 off) + WorkStation Z = $3,099 / 12 = $258.25/month

Application: techstoreplus.com/financing or at point of purchase online/in-store.
Approval decision: within 60 seconds online; same-day in-store.
