TECHSTORE PLUS — POLICY DOCUMENT
TITLE: Return and Exchange Policy
Policy ID: POL-RET-001
Effective January 2024
Supersedes: POL-RET-002 (July 2023)

================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

TechStore Plus is committed to customer satisfaction. If a product does not meet
your expectations, you may return or exchange it under the conditions described
in this policy. This policy applies to all purchases made directly through
TechStore Plus, whether online at techstoreplus.com or in our physical showroom
at New York, USA.

================================================================================
RETURN WINDOWS
================================================================================

EXCHANGES (store credit or same-value product swap)
- Window: 30 calendar days from the delivery date (online orders) or purchase
  date (in-store purchases).
- Condition: item must be in its original packaging with all accessories, manuals,
  and registration cards included. The product must show no physical damage
  beyond normal inspection.
- A restocking fee of 10% applies to opened products. Sealed, unopened products
  are exchanged with no restocking fee.

REFUNDS (return to original payment method)
- Window: 7 calendar days from the delivery date (online) or purchase date
  (in-store).
- Condition: same as exchanges — original packaging, all accessories, no damage.
- Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the returned product.
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a TechStore Plus
  error (wrong item shipped, item arrived damaged).

================================================================================
CONDITION REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

To qualify for a return or exchange, the product must meet ALL of the following:
1. Original, unmodified retail packaging (box, inserts, protective film if not
   removed during setup).
2. All bundled accessories: cables, adapters, manuals, warranty cards.
3. No physical damage caused by the customer: no cracks, dents, scratches from
   use, or liquid exposure.
4. No evidence of unauthorized repair, modification, or flashing of firmware/BIOS.
5. Serial number label must be intact and legible.

Products failing one or more conditions are ineligible for return and will be
returned to the customer at their expense.

================================================================================
NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
================================================================================

The following categories are non-returnable once opened:
- Software licenses (product keys, digital downloads)
- Consumables (printer ink, batteries sold individually)
- Personalized or custom-configured products
- Products purchased under a special "Final Sale" promotion (clearly marked at
  time of purchase)
- Hygiene-sensitive peripherals (in-ear headphones, microphone covers) once
  opened

================================================================================
HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN
================================================================================

ONLINE ORDERS
1. Log in at techstoreplus.com and navigate to Orders > [Order Number] > Return.
2. Select the item(s) to return and choose the reason.
3. Print the prepaid return shipping label (available for orders over $100 with
   defective products; standard returns require customer-paid shipping).
4. Pack the item securely in the original box and drop it at the carrier's
   nearest location.
5. TechStore Plus will email a refund confirmation within 2 business days of
   receiving the item.

IN-STORE RETURNS
1. Bring the product, proof of purchase (receipt or order email), and all
   accessories to any TechStore Plus showroom.
2. A sales associate will inspect the product on the spot.
3. Eligible refunds are processed to the original payment method same day (credit
   card refunds may take 3–5 business days to reflect on your statement).

================================================================================
DEFECTIVE PRODUCT RETURNS
================================================================================

If a product arrives damaged or develops a defect within 30 days of purchase,
TechStore Plus covers return shipping and offers a full refund, exchange, or
equivalent replacement at no additional cost. This is separate from the warranty
process. After 30 days, defects are handled through the warranty — see
policy_warranty_terms.txt and support_warranty_claim_process.txt.
