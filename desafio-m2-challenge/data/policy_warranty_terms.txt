================================================================================
CHANGES FROM PREVIOUS VERSION (v2.0, 2023)
================================================================================

This v3.0 policy amends the 2023 warranty terms in the following material ways:

1. PREMIUM TIER — ACCIDENTAL DAMAGE (NEW in v3.0):
   The Premium Protection Plan now includes accidental damage coverage (drops,
   spills, cracks) for ALL Premium tier products, including Laptop Pro X1,
   WorkStation Z, and Router NX300. Under v2.0 (2023), no tier covered accidental
   damage. This is the most significant change in this policy update.
   One incident per 12-month period; $49 service fee applies per incident.

2. PREMIUM TIER — WATER DAMAGE (NEW in v3.0):
   The Premium Protection Plan now includes liquid and water damage coverage for
   ALL Premium tier products, including networking equipment (Router NX300).
   Under v2.0 (2023), water damage was excluded for all products and all tiers.

3. STANDARD TIER — DURATION EXTENDED (changed in v3.0):
   Standard Warranty Extension duration increased from 24 months to 30 months
   total coverage. This applies to products purchased on or after January 1, 2024.
   Products purchased before January 1, 2024 retain the 24-month Standard duration
   under the original v2.0 (2023) terms.

4. BASIC TIER: No change from v2.0 (2023). Basic tier remains 12 months, with no
   accidental damage and no water damage coverage.

DETERMINING WHICH POLICY APPLIES: The version of this policy that applies to a
specific product is determined by the product's original purchase date.
- Purchased January 1, 2023 – December 31, 2023: covered under v2.0 (2023)
- Purchased January 1, 2024 or later: covered under this v3.0 (2024)

================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

TechStore Plus provides a Limited Warranty on all hardware products sold through
its channels (online and in-store). This document defines the scope, duration,
exclusions, and claim process for each warranty tier. Three warranty tiers are
available, as described below.

================================================================================
WARRANTY TIERS — VERSION 3.0 (2024)
================================================================================

TIER 1 — BASIC WARRANTY
- Duration: 12 months from the date of purchase.
- Coverage: manufacturing defects and hardware component failures under normal use.
- Does NOT cover: accidental damage, liquid/water damage, cosmetic damage (dents,
  scratches), unauthorized modifications, consumable degradation.
- Eligible products: all TechStore Plus hardware at no additional charge.
- Monthly cost: $0.00 (included with purchase).
- Products at this tier: Laptop Lite V3, Smart Cam 4K, Smart Speaker Mini,
  Smart Plug 2-pack.
- UNCHANGED from v2.0 (2023).

TIER 2 — STANDARD WARRANTY EXTENSION
- Duration: 30 months total (12 months Basic + 18 months extension).
  NOTE: Under v2.0 (2023), this was 24 months total. The 2024 update extended it
  to 30 months for products purchased on or after January 1, 2024.
- Coverage: everything in Tier 1 plus priority service (next available technician,
  no queue wait) and free on-site diagnosis for laptops.
- Does NOT cover: accidental damage, liquid damage. (Unchanged from v2.0.)
- Eligible products: all TechStore Plus hardware.
- Must be purchased within 90 days of the original product purchase.
- Monthly cost: $4.99 per product.
- Products at this tier: Laptop Air S2, Smart Hub Home, Laptop Pro X0 (legacy).

TIER 3 — PREMIUM PROTECTION PLAN
- Duration: 36 months total (12 months Basic + 24 months Premium extension).
  Unchanged from v2.0 (2023).
- Coverage: everything in Tier 2 PLUS accidental damage (drops, spills, cracks)
  AND liquid/water damage. This coverage extension is NEW in v3.0 (2024).
  Under v2.0 (2023), Premium tier covered hardware failure ONLY — no accidental
  or water damage coverage existed under any tier.
- Accidental damage limit: one incident per 12-month period. A $49 service fee
  applies per incident.
- Eligible products: laptops, tablets, smartphones, smart home devices,
  and networking equipment (routers). Accidental damage coverage for networking
  equipment (Router NX300) is included as of this v3.0 (2024) update.
- Must be purchased within 90 days of the original product purchase.
- Monthly cost: $9.99 per product.
- Products at this tier: Laptop Pro X1, WorkStation Z, Router NX300.

================================================================================
WHAT THE WARRANTY COVERS (ALL TIERS)
================================================================================

- Manufacturing defects: components that fail during normal use without external
  physical cause.
- Dead pixels: display panels with more than 3 dead pixels (stuck or dark) within
  the warranty period.
- Battery defect: cells that fail to hold any charge within 12 months of purchase.
  NOTE: battery capacity degradation below 80% is expected wear after 12 months
  and is NOT covered by Tier 1. Tier 3 (Premium) covers battery replacement once
  per year if capacity falls below 70%.
- Port failure: USB, HDMI, audio, or proprietary ports that stop functioning
  without physical damage.
- Keyboard/trackpad failure: hardware malfunction, not software or driver issues.
- Accidental damage (Tier 3 only, NEW in v3.0): drops, spills, cracks — one
  incident per 12-month period, $49 service fee per incident.
- Water/liquid damage (Tier 3 only, NEW in v3.0): applies to all Premium tier
  products including Router NX300.

================================================================================
WHAT THE WARRANTY DOES NOT COVER (ALL TIERS UNLESS NOTED)
================================================================================

- Physical damage caused by the user (dropping, bending, crushing) — covered by
  Tier 3 only (with $49 fee per incident) as of v3.0 (2024). NOT covered by any
  tier under v2.0 (2023).
- Liquid or water damage — covered by Tier 3 only (with $49 fee) as of v3.0
  (2024). NOT covered by any tier under v2.0 (2023).
- Damage from using non-TechStore Plus power adapters rated outside the product's
  specified wattage.
- Software issues, viruses, malware, OS corruption.
- Cosmetic damage not affecting function (scratches, dents, discoloration).
- Consumables: replacement chargers, replacement cables, cleaning accessories.
- Products with removed, altered, or illegible serial number labels.
- Products repaired by unauthorized third parties (warranty void upon unauthorized
  disassembly).
- Damage caused by acts of God: fire, flood, lightning strikes, earthquakes.

================================================================================
REPAIR, REPLACEMENT, AND REFUND POLICY
================================================================================

When a covered claim is approved, TechStore Plus will, at its sole discretion:
1. REPAIR the product using original or equivalent-quality parts, or
2. REPLACE the product with a refurbished or new unit of equal or greater
   specifications, or
3. REFUND the original purchase price if repair and replacement are not feasible.

TechStore Plus covers all shipping costs for warranty service. Standard repair
turnaround time is 5-10 business days after receipt of the product.

================================================================================
HOW TO FILE A CLAIM
================================================================================

See support_warranty_claim_process.txt for the complete step-by-step process.
In summary:
1. Go to techstoreplus.com/claims or call 1-800-TECH-PLUS.
2. Provide your proof of purchase and serial number.
3. Describe the defect. TechStore Plus will respond within 1 business day.

When filing a claim, TechStore Plus support will verify your original purchase date
to determine whether Warranty Terms v2.0 (2023) or this v3.0 (2024) policy applies.
Products purchased before January 1, 2024 are evaluated under the archived v2.0
terms in policy_warranty_terms_2023.txt.

================================================================================
TRANSFERABILITY
================================================================================

TechStore Plus warranties are non-transferable. They apply only to the original
purchaser. If you sell or give away the product, the remaining warranty coverage
does not transfer to the new owner.

================================================================================
LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
================================================================================

TechStore Plus's liability under this warranty is limited to the lesser of the
cost of repair, replacement, or refund of the original product purchase price.
TechStore Plus is not liable for data loss — always back up your data before
submitting a device for service.
