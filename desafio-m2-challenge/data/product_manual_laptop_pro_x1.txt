OVERVIEW
================================================================================

The Laptop Pro X1 is TechStore Plus's flagship productivity laptop, designed for
professionals who need sustained performance across demanding workloads. It ships
with 16 GB of Samsung LPDDR5 RAM, a 512 GB NVMe SSD, a 15.6-inch IPS display at
1920x1080, an Intel Core i7-13th Gen (i7-1355U) processor, Intel Iris Xe
integrated graphics, and a 60 Wh battery rated for up to 10 hours of mixed use.

Connectivity: 2x USB-A 3.2, 2x USB-C (one with Thunderbolt 4), 1x HDMI 2.0,
1x 3.5mm combo audio jack, Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax), Bluetooth 5.2, SD card reader.

Weight: 1.8 kg. Dimensions: 356 x 234 x 18.5 mm.

UPGRADE PATH: The Laptop Pro X1 (2024) replaces the Laptop Pro X0 (2022). Existing
Laptop Pro X0 owners can trade in toward the Laptop Pro X1 through the TechStore
Plus Trade-In Program (see trade_in_program.txt for details). Customers upgrading
from the Laptop Air S2 are also eligible for a trade-in credit toward the Laptop Pro X1.

INITIAL SETUP
================================================================================

1. Remove the laptop from its packaging and place it on a flat, stable surface.
2. Connect the 65W USB-C power adapter to the left USB-C port and plug into a
   grounded outlet. Charge for at least 30 minutes before first boot.
3. Open the lid — the system powers on automatically.
4. Follow the Windows 11 OOBE (Out-of-Box Experience):
   a. Select language, region, and keyboard layout.
   b. Connect to a Wi-Fi network. Skip if you prefer a local account.
   c. Sign in with a Microsoft account or create a local account.
   d. Choose privacy settings. TechStore Plus recommends disabling optional
      telemetry for enterprise environments.
5. Once at the desktop, run Windows Update (Settings > Windows Update > Check
   for updates). Install all critical updates and restart when prompted.
6. Register your warranty at techstoreplus.com/warranty using the serial number
   found on the bottom label (format: TSP-XXXXXXXX).

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

ISSUE: Laptop does not power on
- Ensure the power adapter is fully seated in the USB-C port and the outlet is
  live. Try a different outlet.
- Hold the Power button for 10 seconds to force a hard reset.
- If the charging LED does not illuminate at all, contact TechStore Plus support.

ISSUE: Docking Station Pro not detected
- Ensure you are using the left USB-C port (Thunderbolt 4), not the right USB-C port.
- Update Thunderbolt firmware: Device Manager > Firmware > Thunderbolt(TM) Controller.
- Try a different Thunderbolt 4 cable (the included 65W cable works; third-party
  cables may lack full Thunderbolt 4 negotiation).

ISSUE: USB-C Hub 7-in-1 HDMI limited to 1080p
- The USB-C Hub 7-in-1 supports HDMI 4K@30Hz. If output is capped at 1080p, check
  display cable. For 4K@60Hz, use the Docking Station Pro instead.

ISSUE: Display shows artifacts or lines
- Update Intel graphics drivers via Device Manager or intel.com/support.
- Perform a hard reset (hold Power 10 s). If the issue persists after reboot,
  the display may require service — contact support@techstoreplus.com.

ISSUE: Battery drains faster than expected
- Open Settings > System > Power & battery > Battery saver.
- Check for background apps using excessive CPU via Task Manager (Ctrl+Shift+Esc).
- Calibrate the battery: fully charge, then fully discharge in Power Saver mode,
  then fully recharge.

ISSUE: Wi-Fi drops or shows limited connectivity
- Go to Settings > Network & Internet > Wi-Fi > Hardware Properties and set
  Preferred band to 5 GHz.
- Update the Wi-Fi driver via Device Manager.
- For best results, use the Laptop Pro X1 with the Router NX300, which supports
  Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) for maximum throughput and lower latency.
