CHANGES IN THE 2024 UPDATE (v3.0 AMENDS v2.0)
================================================================================

The following changes were made in Warranty Terms v3.0 (effective January 1, 2024)
compared to this archived v2.0 (2023) document:

1. PREMIUM TIER — ACCIDENTAL DAMAGE: Not covered under v2.0 (2023). NOW COVERED
   under v3.0 (2024) for all Premium tier products, including Laptop Pro X1,
   WorkStation Z, and Router NX300. This is the most significant change.

2. PREMIUM TIER — WATER DAMAGE: Not covered under v2.0 (2023). NOW COVERED
   under v3.0 (2024) for all Premium tier products including the Router NX300.
   Under v2.0, no tier covered water damage for networking equipment.

3. STANDARD TIER — DURATION: Extended from 24 months (v2.0) to 30 months (v3.0).
   Products purchased before January 1, 2024 under Standard tier receive 24 months.
   Products purchased on or after January 1, 2024 under Standard tier receive 30 months.

4. BASIC TIER: Unchanged between v2.0 and v3.0 — 12 months, no accidental coverage,
   no water damage coverage.

These changes are relevant for multi-hop warranty queries: a product purchased
in 2023 and covered by this archived v2.0 policy does NOT have accidental or water
damage coverage under Premium tier, even if the current product page advertises
v3.0 coverage. The coverage that applies is determined by purchase date.

OVERVIEW
================================================================================

TechStore Plus provides a Limited Warranty on all hardware products sold through
its channels (online and in-store). This document defines the scope, duration,
exclusions, and claim process for each warranty tier as applicable to products
purchased between January 1, 2023 and December 31, 2023. Three warranty tiers
were available under this version of the policy.

================================================================================
WARRANTY TIERS — VERSION 2.0 (2023)
================================================================================

TIER 1 — BASIC WARRANTY (v2.0, 2023)
- Duration: 12 months from the date of purchase.
- Coverage: manufacturing defects and hardware component failures under normal use.
- Does NOT cover: accidental damage, liquid/water damage, cosmetic damage (dents,
  scratches), unauthorized modifications, consumable degradation.
- Eligible products: all TechStore Plus hardware at no additional charge.
- Monthly cost: $0.00 (included with purchase).
- Products covered at this tier in 2023: Laptop Lite V3, Smart Cam 4K, Smart
  Speaker Mini, Smart Plug 2-pack.

TIER 2 — STANDARD WARRANTY EXTENSION (v2.0, 2023)
- Duration: 24 months total (12 months Basic + 12 months extension).
  NOTE: v3.0 (2024) extended this to 30 months for new purchases.
- Coverage: everything in Tier 1 plus priority service (next available technician,
  no queue wait) and free on-site diagnosis for laptops.
- Does NOT cover: accidental damage, liquid damage.
- Eligible products: all TechStore Plus hardware.
- Must be purchased within 90 days of the original product purchase.
- Monthly cost: $4.99 per product.
- Products covered at this tier in 2023: Laptop Air S2, Laptop Pro X0, Smart Hub Home.

TIER 3 — PREMIUM PROTECTION PLAN (v2.0, 2023)
- Duration: 36 months total (12 months Basic + 24 months Premium extension).
- Coverage: everything in Tier 2 PLUS... HARDWARE FAILURE ONLY FOR ALL PRODUCTS.
  IMPORTANT DIFFERENCE FROM v3.0 (2024): Under this v2.0 (2023) policy, the
  Premium tier does NOT cover accidental damage (drops, spills, cracks).
  Under this v2.0 (2023) policy, the Premium tier does NOT cover liquid or
  water damage for any product category, including networking equipment.
  This is a critical distinction: a Laptop Pro X1 purchased in 2023 under the
  Premium tier is covered for hardware failures only — not drops, not spills.
  The 2024 update (v3.0) changed this to include accidental and water damage.
- Eligible products: laptops, tablets, smartphones, smart home devices,
  networking equipment (hardware failure only).
- Must be purchased within 90 days of the original product purchase.
- Monthly cost: $9.99 per product.
- Products covered at this tier in 2023: Laptop Pro X1 (2023 build), Router NX300
  (note: Router NX300 launched January 2024, so no 2023 purchases exist for it),
  Router NX200, WorkStation Z.

================================================================================
WHAT THE WARRANTY COVERS (ALL TIERS, v2.0 2023)
================================================================================

- Manufacturing defects: components that fail during normal use without external
  physical cause.
- Dead pixels: display panels with more than 3 dead pixels within the warranty period.
- Battery defect: cells that fail to hold any charge within 12 months of purchase.
  NOTE: battery capacity degradation below 80% is expected wear and is NOT covered.
- Port failure: USB, HDMI, audio, or proprietary ports that stop functioning
  without physical damage.
- Keyboard/trackpad failure: hardware malfunction, not software or driver issues.

================================================================================
WHAT THE WARRANTY DOES NOT COVER (ALL TIERS, v2.0 2023)
================================================================================

- Physical damage caused by the user (dropping, bending, crushing) — NOT covered
  under any tier in this v2.0 policy. (v3.0 added Premium tier coverage.)
- Liquid or water damage — NOT covered under any tier in this v2.0 policy.
  (v3.0 added Premium tier coverage.)
- Damage from using non-TechStore Plus power adapters rated outside specification.
- Software issues, viruses, malware, OS corruption.
- Cosmetic damage not affecting function (scratches, dents, discoloration).
- Consumables: replacement chargers, replacement cables, cleaning accessories.
- Products with removed, altered, or illegible serial number labels.
- Products repaired by unauthorized third parties.
- Damage caused by acts of God: fire, flood, lightning strikes, earthquakes.

================================================================================
REPAIR, REPLACEMENT, AND REFUND POLICY (v2.0 2023)
================================================================================

When a covered claim is approved, TechStore Plus will, at its sole discretion:
1. REPAIR the product using original or equivalent-quality parts, or
2. REPLACE the product with a refurbished or new unit of equal or greater
   specifications, or
3. REFUND the original purchase price if repair and replacement are not feasible.

TechStore Plus covers all shipping costs for warranty service. Standard repair
turnaround time is 5-10 business days after receipt of the product.

================================================================================
HOW TO FILE A CLAIM (v2.0 2023)
================================================================================

The claim process is the same under both v2.0 and v3.0 policies:
See support_warranty_claim_process.txt for the complete step-by-step process.
In summary:
1. Go to techstoreplus.com/claims or call 1-800-TECH-PLUS.
2. Provide your proof of purchase and serial number.
3. Describe the defect. TechStore Plus will respond within 1 business day.
Note: When filing a claim, TechStore Plus support will verify your purchase date
to determine whether v2.0 (2023) or v3.0 (2024) terms apply.
