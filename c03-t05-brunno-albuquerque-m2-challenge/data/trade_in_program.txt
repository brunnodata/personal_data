================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

The TechStore Plus Trade-In Program allows customers to exchange eligible
TechStore Plus products for a credit that can be applied toward the purchase of
a newer TechStore Plus product. Trade-in credits are non-cash and must be applied
at the time of purchase. Credits cannot be combined with promotional discounts
unless otherwise stated.

The Trade-In Program is eligible for combining with TechStore Plus Financing
(0% APR, 12 monthly installments). See the Financing section below for examples.

================================================================================
ELIGIBLE TRADE-IN PRODUCTS AND CREDIT VALUES
================================================================================

LAPTOPS

  Laptop Air S2 (SKU: LA-S2-2023) — Trade-in credit: $200
    Eligible upgrade destinations: Laptop Pro X1 OR WorkStation Z
    The Laptop Air S2 is eligible for a $200 trade-in credit toward the purchase
    of either the Laptop Pro X1 ($1,299) or the WorkStation Z ($3,299).
    After trade-in credit: Laptop Pro X1 net cost = $1,099 | WorkStation Z net = $3,099.

    Laptop Air S2, then separately trade in the Laptop Air S2 toward the Laptop Pro X1.

PRODUCTS NOT ELIGIBLE FOR TRADE-IN

The following current products are NOT eligible to be traded in (they are traded
INTO, not out of):

  - WorkStation Z (WS-Z-2024) — current flagship desktop
  - Router NX300 (RT-NX300-2024) — current flagship router
  - Any accessory (Docking Station Pro, USB-C Hub 7-in-1, Thunderbolt 4 Hub, etc.)
  - Smart Cam 4K, Smart Speaker Mini, Smart Plug 2-pack

================================================================================
COMBINING TRADE-IN WITH FINANCING
================================================================================

Trade-in credits can be combined with TechStore Plus Financing (0% APR, 12 monthly
installments, available on purchases over $500). The trade-in credit is applied
as a down payment, reducing the financed amount.

Example 1 — Laptop Air S2 trade-in + Laptop Pro X1 + Financing:
  Laptop Pro X1 price:          $1,299
  Laptop Air S2 trade-in:       - $200
  Net financed amount:          $1,099
  Monthly payment (12 months):  $91.58/month at 0% APR

Example 2 — Laptop Air S2 trade-in + WorkStation Z + Financing:
  WorkStation Z price:          $3,299
  Laptop Air S2 trade-in:       - $200
  Net financed amount:          $3,099
  Monthly payment (12 months):  $258.25/month at 0% APR

Example 3 — Router NX200 trade-in + Router NX300 (no financing needed):
  Router NX300 price:           $199
  Router NX200 trade-in:        - $50
  Amount due at purchase:       $149 (below $500 financing threshold)

================================================================================
TRADE-IN PROCESS
================================================================================

1. Initiate the trade-in online at techstoreplus.com/trade-in or at any
   TechStore Plus retail location.
2. Provide the serial number of the product being traded in.
3. TechStore Plus will assess the device condition:
   - Good condition (no cracked screen, no missing parts): full credit value.
   - Fair condition (minor cosmetic damage): 75% of credit value.
   - Poor condition (significant damage, non-functional): 50% of credit value.
4. Accept the credit offer. The credit is applied immediately to a qualifying
   purchase or held on your account for up to 60 days.
5. Ship the trade-in device using the prepaid label provided by TechStore Plus.
   Devices must be received within 14 days of credit offer acceptance.

================================================================================
TERMS AND CONDITIONS
================================================================================

- Trade-in credits are non-transferable and non-refundable.
- Credits cannot be exchanged for cash.
- Products must be original TechStore Plus purchases (verified by serial number).
- Trade-in program values are subject to change. The values listed in this document
  are current as of January 2024.
- Combining with promotions: trade-in credits cannot be combined with bundle
  discounts (Home Office Bundle, Creator Bundle) unless TechStore Plus explicitly
  authorizes a combined offer in writing.
- Financing eligibility: 0% APR financing requires a credit check. Subject to
  approval. Available for purchases over $500.
