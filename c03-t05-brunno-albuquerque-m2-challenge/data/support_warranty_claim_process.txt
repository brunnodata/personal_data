TECHSTORE PLUS — SUPPORT ARTICLE
TITLE: How to Submit a Warranty Claim
Article ID: SUP-WRN-001
Effective January 2024
Applies to: All TechStore Plus products under active warranty

================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

TechStore Plus offers a 12-month standard warranty on all products purchased
through our store (online or showroom). If your product develops a defect covered
by the warranty within the coverage period, you are entitled to a repair, a
replacement unit of equal or greater specifications, or — in cases where neither
is possible — a full refund of the original purchase price.

This article describes the complete warranty claim process, including required
documents, decision timeline, and escalation options.

================================================================================
ELIGIBILITY CHECK — BEFORE YOU FILE
================================================================================

Your claim is eligible if ALL of the following are true:
1. The product was purchased directly from TechStore Plus (receipt or order
   confirmation required).
2. The purchase date is within the warranty period:
   - Standard Warranty: 12 months from purchase date
   - Extended Warranty: 24 months from purchase date
   - Premium Protection Plan: 36 months from purchase date
3. The defect is covered. Covered issues include:
   - Manufacturing defects (components that fail without physical damage)
   - Hardware failure under normal use
   - Defective display, battery, or port under normal use conditions
4. The defect is NOT in the exclusions list:
   - Physical damage (cracked screen, bent chassis)
   - Liquid damage (any exposure to moisture)
   - Damage from unauthorized modifications or third-party repairs
   - Normal wear and consumable degradation (e.g., battery capacity below 80%
     after 12 months is expected and not covered by the Standard Warranty)

If you have the Premium Protection Plan, accidental damage and liquid damage ARE
covered — see policy_warranty_terms.txt for full exclusion details.

================================================================================
REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
================================================================================

Prepare the following before submitting:
1. Proof of purchase: original receipt, order confirmation email, or invoice
   showing the purchase date, product model, and TechStore Plus as the seller.
2. Product serial number: found on the product label (bottom of laptop, back of
   router, bottom of hub — format varies by product, e.g., TSP-XXXXXXXX).
3. Defect description: a clear written description of the issue and the steps
   you have already taken to troubleshoot (refer to the support articles in
   data/support_*). If you have photos or a short video of the defect, attach them
   — this speeds up assessment significantly.
4. Warranty registration confirmation (optional but recommended): if you
   registered at techstoreplus.com/warranty, include the confirmation number.

================================================================================
HOW TO SUBMIT A CLAIM
================================================================================

OPTION A — Online (fastest, 24/7 availability)
1. Go to techstoreplus.com/claims.
2. Log in with your TechStore Plus account or enter your order number.
3. Select the product and click "File Warranty Claim."
4. Upload proof of purchase and defect photos/video.
5. Describe the defect in the text field (minimum 50 words; more detail = faster
   assessment).
6. Submit. You will receive an acknowledgment email within 15 minutes.

OPTION B — Phone
1. Call 1-800-TECH-PLUS (Mon–Fri 9:00–18:00, Sat 10:00–14:00).
2. Say "warranty claim" at the voice prompt.
3. A support agent will guide you through the same steps and create the claim
   on your behalf. Have your serial number and proof of purchase ready.

OPTION C — In-store
1. Visit a TechStore Plus showroom with the product and proof of purchase.
2. A technician will perform an initial assessment on the spot.
3. Eligible claims may be resolved same-day if parts are in stock.

================================================================================
TIMELINES AND DECISIONS
================================================================================

After submission, TechStore Plus will:
- Acknowledge your claim within 1 business day.
- Complete initial assessment within 3 business days (online/phone) or same day
  (in-store walk-in).
- Notify you of the decision — approved, denied, or escalated for further
  technical review — within 5 business days.

If approved:
- REPAIR: Send the product to the address in the approval email. Estimated
  repair time: 5–10 business days after receipt. Return shipping is free.
- REPLACEMENT: A replacement unit ships within 3 business days of approval.
  You return the defective unit using the prepaid label included in the package.
- REFUND: Processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

If denied:
- You will receive a written explanation. Out-of-warranty repairs can be quoted
  separately; call 1-800-TECH-PLUS to request a repair quote.

================================================================================
ESCALATION
================================================================================

If you believe your claim was incorrectly denied:
1. Reply to the denial email and request a secondary review, including any
   additional evidence.
2. A senior technician will re-examine the claim within 5 business days.
3. If still unresolved, you may escalate to TechStore Plus Customer Relations at
   relations@techstoreplus.com.
